Our "Sundae" Combinations

Because we have one called "Monday"

Sashimi Sundae

A Sundae with a fishy twist. Topped with fresh, wild-caught sashimi and eel sauce.

Pulled-Pork Sundae

For the meat lover, this has pulled pork that's been smoked for 96 hours over ethically sourced hickory wood.

Classic Sundae

Take trip down memory lane with this traditional Sundae.

Vegan Sundae

Stay cruelty free with our Whole-Foods cruelty-free Sunday. 100% Soy-milk ice-cream with roasted edamame beans and almonds sprinkled on top. Add a kick with some recycled coffee grounds!

Meat Sundae

If you need more than just pulled pork, you can enjoy two massive meatballs instead of icecream, drizzle with a mustard based barbeque sauce, and served with fries for dipping. Warning: A straw won't work with this one!

Chocolate Sundae

There's no such thing as "too much" chocolate. Enjoy cholocate ice cream, chocolate sprinkles, chocolote whipped cream, chocolate syrup, and chocolate marshmallows. A full piece of 100% cacao dark chocolate tops this masterpiece.

Coffee Sundae

Forget those sugary energy drinks at the convenience store - this is all you'll need. Freshly ground, locally sourced coffee is infused in the vanilla bean ice cream, sprinkled with coffee nips. Note: Decaf is not available (or acceptable).

Mondae Sundae

Got the Monday blues? Drown your sorrows with this earl-grey ice cream, topped with roasted Jasmine leaf and cardemum sprinkles. Sunday is over.
